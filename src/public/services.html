Requirements for work Visa

  • Passport ( front 2 pages and next 2 pages)
  • Matric ( Transcript and Degree)
  • Intermediate ( Transcript and Degree )
  • Bachelors ( Transcript and Degree )
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Bank Statement ( It should be 5 million rupees )
  • 2 Reference or Recommendation Letters
  • 2 Experience Letters
  • IELTS Certificate ( IELTS requirements based on university requirements )
